Skyjo Rules

  1. The game is played with a deck of cards, which includes number cards from -2 to 12.
  2. Each player starts with a board of cards turned face down, the goal is to discover all your cards and that the sum of them is inferior to the other players.
  3. On your turn, you can either draw a card from the deck or take the top card from the discard pile.
  4. You can then play that card by replacing any of your board card with it or discard it and turn one of your cards face up.
  5. When you have a column of identical cards they all turn 0.
  6. The round ends when one player has all his cards face up, then each player except him can play one last turn.
  7. Points are scored based on the number of cards remaining in each player's board, and the player with the lowest score at the end of the game wins.
  8. The game can be played with 2-8 players.
  9. After 10 minutes of inactivity the game is deleted
